A: “Why are you single?! It is not okay for you to be single. It is not okay for you to be so self focused. Hurry up and find someone other than yourself to defer to.”

B: “Okay.” (B becomes a devoted partner to some Significant Other).

A: “What’s wrong with you?! Why are you so enmeshed with your significant other?! You need to be broken up with so that you can learn to stand on your own two feet for a change.”

B: “Okay.” (B is not longer in a relationship with some Significant Other and becomes an avid philosopher who spends most of its time meditating in some cave and attains higher states of consciousness and insights into the nature of existence that makes human affairs seem relatively insignificant).

Return to the top of this dialogue until max_number_of_tries is attained. Then have B murdered, maimed, and/or incarcerated.

The absurdist dialogue above is an allegory of some other phenomenon I have been personally experiencing: the way many people have treated me as though I deserve to be tortured and belittled incessantly until and unless I become embroiled in some passive and socially conformist activity where I do little more than bitch about what a victim I “have to be” (and when I refuse to play up what a victim other people insist that I “have to be”, those people collude against me to force me to act more emotionally disregulated, small minded, and sinister than I really am. Then those people can justify torturing and belittling me further for failing to maintain my stoic demeanor in their midst).

Perhaps I am exagerating. For the sake of minimizing drama, I will simply say that I am exaggerating and not try very hard to make other people see me in any particular way. Anyway…

It does seem to be the case that human adults (especially female-bodied human adults) are coerced by society to be in a perpetual state of abandonment anxiety and/or sense of having to be on a mad search to find a partner to “complete” them (because the economy is rigged to make it very difficult for people to live alone and abortion is now a crime in the United States of America). I have personally never been so surprised to see how aggressively anti-individualistic and anti-intellectual many people are. It seems that, whenever I do anything other than sulk in a corner or act incapacitated with anxiety about the future and how little control I am “supposed to” feel that I have, people go out of their way to criticize me, get in my personal space, and even vandalize and steal my possessions. (I suppose I should be profusely grateful for the fact that no one (at least as I can recall) has ever seriously physically beaten me up).

I know, I know. “Law of Attraction” says that I am implicitly inviting harm to come my way just for verbally expressing the fantasies and apparent memories I have about being “mistreated” by other human beings. (Oh. I forgot. I never ever get mistreated by other people since other people have no free will and other people are automatons controlled by the mind-control matrix which pervades every corner of every space humans are found on Planet Earth, and other people threaten to wreck havoc on the ecosystem, economy, and preservation of cultural artifacts if they are not always treated as the innocent victim in every situation and always praised as being heroic, doing the best they can, et cetera even though such flattery is never sufficient to make up for the fact that such flattery is mostly hyperbolic; mostly lies to pacify the irritable and earily-triggered masses of simpletons).

Who are my authorities?! Aliens from a region of the universe beyond Planet Earth are my authorities. Even if no other humans besides myself understand why and how, I will continue to proclaim that what I do is in subservience to aliens who I have personally contacted and am still in contact with. (I have had contact with aliens before I was eight years old in my bedroom while in a trance after having left the window open. The next day other people apparently reported seeing a similar type of alien on the national news: white skin, big black eyes which are black in the parts they would be white in humans, light brown irises).
